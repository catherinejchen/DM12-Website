  ANCIENT EGYPT (restoration)

In this assignment, previous knowledge regarding Burn/Dodge/Clone tools were used to restore an Ancient Egyptian artifact.

With the use of Photoshop, we used the tools to copy various sections of an image and put it on another. Pattern brush was also made. Aside from these tools, a darker black brush was used to restore certain areas of the image.

This assignment was relatively easy to do but insanely time consuming.
