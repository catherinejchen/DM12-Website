About Catherine Chen
Currently, I am a grade eleven student. I enjoy both arts and sciences/mathematics. For instance, I enjoy drawing and editing photos, and I play more than five instruments (of those include: alto saxophone, tenor saxophone, flute, violin, ukulele, piano and more). At the same time, I enjoy math as well as computer science and chemistry. I have played piano every since I was five (approximately eleven years now) and I completed the ARCT exam in the summer of 2016. However, I only finished the ARCT theory in the summer of 2017 where I took both Advanced History and Advanced Harmony at the same time. These passions are evident in my jobs. I work at Kumon Happy Learning Centre where I act as an instructor (mark and teach) for Early Learners. Besides Kumon, I also work as a violin and piano tutor as well as a theory teacher. I started doing this from around grade eight. Currently, I have three theory students and depending on the week, I could have up to three piano students as well.
