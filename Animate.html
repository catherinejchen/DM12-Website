BLACK AND WHITE IMAGES

An image was first edited in Photoshop to display various colour channels before it was put on animate to create a tutorial. The tutorial on flash explains how to turn images into black and white and how to leave a certain part of the photo untouched (left coloured).

These types of image are extremely appealing to me as it leaves just a spot of colour that is vibrant. This is an interesting technique to learn on Photoshop.		  
 

COKE CAN

A coke can was created through photoshop using a variety of tools (mainly the gaussian blur). Lots of shading was used and a tutorial was followed. Droplets of water were also added to create an even more realistic feel.

This assignment is probably one of my favourite assignments because I enjoy producing a final product that looks cool.

 



 

 

 

  

PHOTO GALLERY

A photo gallery was created with the use of ActionScript in Animate. The overall theme is artwork. This assignment uses a variety of techniques to get the photos including scanning and just physically taking the photo. The photos were edited and the file sizes were compressed before they were put on Animate to produce the collage.

These photos are an accumulation of drawings I have done previously throughout the years.

  
 


 

SMART OBJECTS

A photo collage was created with the assistance of smart objects. These objects were copied and pasted. Going into the object, one change on one image would then be immediately seen on all the images that were connected to the object.

This was done on Photoshop before going onto Animate to create a tutorial on how the final product was created. Image editing was done in Photoshop as well as replication.
